Artwork

Final project of my "UX/UI Design course". The idea was to create a social network for artists. To do so, we make a research about the topic, we also made surveys and interviews to potential users of the app. Sketches and prototypes were also made to test the functionality of the app. This project was divided in 2 parts: One for the UX research and one for the UI Design.

Note: To watch the video in good quality, we recommend to watch this portfolio on pc or tablet.

User Experience (UX)

For the UX part, we could see 3 different steps:

  • 1) Observation: On this step, we made a research about apps that look alike (Desk Research), about different opinions that the users have about them (Netnography), using graphics to visualize our conclusions. We also started to consider options about how to approach the project using "Research Questions", "Benchmarking" and a "SWOT analysis". A survey was also made using "Google Forms" and we made some interviews to potential users of the app (Both artists and non artists).

User Experience (UX)

  • 2)Summary: On this step we analysed the profile of the potential users of the app by creating a "User Persona", we also used "Empathy Maps", "Customer Journeys" and a "Matrix of Needs".
  • 3)Ideation: We organised the ideas gathered in the previous steps and we analyse their viability through "MoSCoW diagrams", "in/Out diagrams". We also made "Value Propositions" from the ideas. In this phase, we choose which ideas will be added or discarded from the project.

User Interface (UI)

This part is about creating a functional prototype of the app. We started with a "Moodboard", we looked for references and inspiration from similar projects. Then we created "flowcharts" and "wireframes" for the basic structure of the app. We also created a high-fidelity prototype using "Figma". We designed the minimal elements using this software so we could design the prototype in a easier and more efficient way.
